Our Mission

The Crediton Cemetery Heritage Portal is dedicated to preserving and sharing the rich history of Crediton Cemetery. Through digitizing records, creating virtual exhibits, and engaging the community, we aim to honor the memory of those buried here and ensure their stories endure.

Connect people through volunteering, events, and contributions.

Our History

Crediton Cemetery was established in the early 1830s as settlers, many of German descent, began to make Stephen Township their home. The first recorded burial, Barbara Shulz in 1835, marks the beginning of a legacy that spans nearly two centuries. Over time, the cemetery became a resting place for pioneers, veterans, and community members, each headstone telling a story of resilience and heritage.

In 2020, a group of dedicated volunteers launched the Crediton Cemetery Heritage Portal to preserve these stories digitally. Today, we continue to expand our efforts with the support of donors, volunteers, and the community, ensuring this historic site remains a cherished resource.
