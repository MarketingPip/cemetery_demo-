How to Navigate the Family Relationship Chart

Locate your generation (rows 1-9) and another person’s generation (columns 1-9) from a shared ancestor. The intersection reveals your relationship—e.g., a grandson (row 2) to a great-grandson (column 3) is a first cousin once removed. Use this tool to explore your ancestry within Crediton Cemetery.
