Volunteer with Us

Join our team to preserve Crediton Cemetery’s legacy through research, photography, data entry, and event support.

Volunteer Opportunities

We’re looking for passionate individuals to help us maintain and share Crediton’s rich history. Here’s how you can get involved:

Research

Dig into historical records, family histories, and archives to uncover new stories about Crediton’s past.

Photography

Capture images of monuments, landscapes, and events to document and share the cemetery’s beauty.

Data Entry

Help digitize cemetery records and historical documents to make them accessible online.

Event Support

Assist with organizing and running community events, tours, and educational programs.

Why Volunteer?

Your time and skills make a lasting impact. Here’s what you’ll gain and contribute:

Make a Difference

Preserve history and honor the memory of Crediton’s pioneers and veterans.

Learn New Skills

Gain experience in research, photography, or event planning while working with a dedicated team.

Connect with Community

Meet others who share your passion for history and heritage preservation.

Get Involved

Ready to join us? Contact our volunteer coordinator to learn more about current opportunities and how you can contribute.
