Your generosity helps preserve Crediton Cemetery’s history and maintain its grounds for future generations.

How You Can Help

Every donation supports our efforts to maintain the cemetery, digitize records, and create educational exhibits. Choose the method that works best for you.

E-Transfer

Send your donation securely via e-transfer to:

donations@creditoncemetery.org

Please include your name and "Donation" in the message field. We’ll email you a tax receipt.

PayPal

Donate quickly and easily online using PayPal.

Donate with PayPal

Tax receipts will be sent to your PayPal email address.

Donations support digitizing records and protecting artifacts for future generations.

Educational Outreach

Your support enables exhibits and programs that share Crediton’s rich history.
