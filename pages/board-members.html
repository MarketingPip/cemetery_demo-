Board Responsibilities

Cemetery Care

Overseeing the maintenance and preservation of cemetery grounds, monuments, and facilities to ensure they remain respectful places of remembrance.

Historical Preservation

Managing historical records, supporting genealogical research, and promoting awareness of the cemetery's cultural and historical significance.

Community Engagement

Facilitating community connections through educational programs, volunteer opportunities, and commemorative events that honor our shared heritage.

Join Our Efforts

The Crediton Cemetery Board welcomes community involvement. Whether through volunteering, attending events, or supporting our preservation initiatives, your participation helps ensure this important heritage site continues to serve our community.
