Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your information while preserving the heritage of Crediton Cemetery.

Our Privacy Commitment

The Crediton Cemetery Heritage Portal is committed to protecting the privacy of our visitors, volunteers, and donors. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your personal information in accordance with applicable Canadian privacy laws, including the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA).

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, or mailing address when you contact us, volunteer, donate, or submit historical records.
  • Genealogical Information: Details about individuals buried in Crediton Cemetery or their families, provided voluntarily for historical preservation.
  • Website Usage Data: Non-identifiable data such as IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited, collected via cookies or analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Respond to inquiries or provide support.
  • Process donations and issue tax receipts.
  • Enhance our historical records and exhibits with your contributions.
  • Improve our website and understand visitor preferences.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. It may be shared only:

  • With service providers (e.g., payment processors) who assist us, under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • When required by law or to protect the rights, safety, or property of the Crediton Cemetery Heritage Portal.
  • With your consent, such as including genealogical data in public exhibits.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your information, including encryption for online transactions and secure storage for physical records. However, no method is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information we hold.
  • Withdraw consent for use of your data (where applicable).
  • Contact us with privacy concerns or questions.

To exercise these rights, please reach out via our Contact page.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies to enhance your experience. These small files track usage but do not identify you personally. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may limit some features.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last Revised" date. Please check back periodically.

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please don’t hesitate to get in touch. Your privacy is important to us.
