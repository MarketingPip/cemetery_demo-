Facility Features

Scenic Grounds

Our cemetery is nestled in a serene location, perfect for commemorative services and peaceful gatherings.

Features include: beautiful greenery, peaceful pathways, and historical gravestones.

Facilities

The cemetery offers benches and shaded areas, with nearby restroom access. A chapel for ceremonies is also available upon request.

For smaller gatherings, seating can be arranged. Outdoor events can be planned with suitable weather conditions.

Parking & Accessibility

The cemetery offers ample parking spaces, with accessible paths for guests with mobility challenges. There are two accessible parking spots near the chapel entrance.

Paths and driveways are well-maintained to ensure ease of access for all visitors.
