Explore the Crediton Community Centre

Host your event at our versatile facility, perfect for gatherings of all sizes.

Overview of the Crediton Community Centre

The Crediton Community Centre is the perfect venue for a variety of events. From intimate weddings to large fundraisers, we offer all the necessary amenities for a successful gathering.

Facility Features

Washrooms

Male and female washrooms are available on-site, offering convenience and comfort for all guests.

Facility Entrance & Parking

The facility offers two accessible parking spaces, with curb cut access to the entrance and a gentle slope walkway. The entrance includes automatic doors operated by a push button for easy access.

Inside the Community Hall

The hall can accommodate up to 150 people and is ideal for intimate weddings, banquets, showers, programs, and fundraisers. The space is open and well-lit, with a kitchen and bar available for catering services. The hall is available for rental year-round, and a pavilion is available during the summer months.

Inside the Kitchen

Kitchen Equipment

  • Microwave
  • Fridge
  • Cooler
  • Stove
  • Dishwasher

Kitchen Supplies

Guests have access to pots, pans, serving dishes, cutlery, plates, bowls, cups, and mugs to ensure all your catering needs are met.

Ball Diamond & Playground

In addition to the community hall, the facility also includes a ball diamond and playground on the same property, perfect for family-friendly events.

Pricing & Booking
