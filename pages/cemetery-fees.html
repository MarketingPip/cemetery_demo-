Care & Maintenance

Required under the Funeral, Burial and Cremation Services Act, 2002, the following amounts must be paid to the cemetery operator before a marker is installed. Contributions are remitted to an irrevocable cemetery care and maintenance trust fund, with income used for perpetual care and maintenance expenses.

Marker Type Fee
Flat Marker (smaller than 1,116.13 cm² / 173 in²) $0.00
Flat Marker (1,116.13 cm² / 173 in² or larger) $100.00
Upright Marker (1.22m / 4 ft or less in height and length, including base) $200.00
Upright Marker (more than 1.22m / 4 ft in height or length, including base) $400.00
