Lulu Odell Gaiser: A Trailblazer in Science and Education

Discover the life and legacy of Lulu Odell Gaiser, who was born & died in Crediton, Ontario, she became the first woman to join McMaster University’s faculty. A pioneer in plant pathology, her groundbreaking work and advocacy for women in academia continue to inspire generations.
